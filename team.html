Our Team

We are a diverse group of 14 computational biologists, data scientists, statisticians, and researchers united by our passion for advancing cancer research through cutting-edge AI and Large Language Models. Our interdisciplinary team combines expertise in AI/ML, genomics, spatial biology, and clinical translation to tackle pancreatic and breast cancer through innovative computational approaches.
